The meeting focused on discussing potential causes of low energy and sluggishness in the evening, even with adequate sleep and regular eating. Sameer Uddin shared his insights on how food choices, lifestyle, and underlying health conditions such as thyroid function or stress could contribute to this issue. He also proposed simple tweaks to daily routines and offered to help participants plan their schedules. The discussion touched on the importance of nutrition, including consuming high-fiber and protein foods, and avoiding refined carbohydrates and sugary drinks.
